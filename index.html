About Me

Before beginning her journey to become a web developer, Laura worked for twenty five years as a Mechanical Engineer in the defense industry. She worked on a variety of programs ranging from SINCGARS radios to test equipment for optical devices to cabling for GPS satellite panels. She is looking forward to new challenges, and a new career path.


In her free time, Laura enjoys exploring the Austin area, where she has lived for almost a year. Her other hobbies include drinking coffee, taking road trips, cooking, spending time with her family, and reading. She also creates jewelry and decorative pieces using stained glass and fused glass.
